Frequently asked questions

Does Zacky replace Jira, ClickUp, Trello, Asana or MS Project?
Yes. Zacky not only replaces them but surpasses them. While those tools only manage tasks, Zacky gives you live EVM (CPI/SPI), AI Transcriber that automatically creates tasks from meetings, 4D Dashboard with executive vision of all your projects, and 4 dedicated AI Agents.
How does the AI Transcriber work?
Zacky records the meeting (or integrates with Teams/Meet/Zoom) and automatically extracts: complete transcript, executive summary, key points, decisions made, and ACTIONS with responsible persons and dates. Tasks are created automatically in Kanban/Gantt.
What is EVM and why do I need it?
EVM (Earned Value Management) is the PMI standard methodology for measuring project performance. Zacky automatically calculates CPI (Cost Performance Index) and SPI (Schedule Performance Index) in real time.
Can I migrate my data from Jira/ClickUp/Trello/Asana?
Yes. Zacky has a mass importer that converts your data (tasks, projects, dates, responsible persons) from CSV, Excel or direct API from Jira, ClickUp, Trello, Asana and MS Project. The process takes less than 15 minutes.
Is there support in English?
Yes. 24/7 support for Professional and Enterprise plans. We also have personalized onboarding with an assigned Project Manager.
What happens if I cancel my subscription?
You can export all your data in Excel, PDF or JSON format. You won't lose anything. And you will always have read access to your historical projects.
